Deep Rang 2022 - LJ Polytechnic

Celebrating cultural or religious festivals in academic institutions is a learning experience for students who get to learn about the festival. The festivals provide an opportunity for students to respect and participate in events that are not their own. It will also encourage and enhance the true sense of brotherhood and social harmony, bridging the linguistic and regional barriers. And one of the programs held by LJ Poltechnic is The Deep Rang. Held on every Diwali. But on 19th of October, is being held after the gap of 3 years. Here the students of LJ Polytechnic put up different stalls for food, accesories, bevarages, soft drinks, water and the first time in the history of LJ, there is the Game stall. There are 4 game stalls and one of them is Zone 94. Also along with the stalls, The performances and Competetions are also held in the Deep Rang. This Time the program is followed by Garba and Bursting fire crackers
